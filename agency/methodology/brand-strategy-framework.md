# Brand Strategy Framework

## Required outputs
- market context
- target audience
- positioning statement
- differentiation
- tone of voice
- message pillars
- proof and trust anchors
- primary CTA strategy

## Questions to answer
1. Who is the best-fit customer?
2. What problem is most expensive or frustrating for them?
3. What does this business do better or differently?
4. What should visitors remember after leaving the site?
5. What action should the site drive?

## Deliverable target
Write strategy so it can directly inform:
- homepage hero
- navigation
- page hierarchy
- proof sections
- CTA language
