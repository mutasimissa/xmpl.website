# Conversion Rate Optimization Framework

## Goal
Ensure every page on the website contributes to the conversion goal defined in the business model.

## Conversion hierarchy

1. **Primary conversion** — the main business goal (e.g. discovery call, demo request, inquiry)
2. **Micro conversions** — smaller steps that indicate intent (e.g. download, FAQ click, scroll depth)
3. **Engagement signals** — behavioral indicators (e.g. time on page, pages per session)

## Page-level CRO checklist

For every primary page, verify:

- [ ] Clear value proposition visible above the fold
- [ ] Primary CTA visible without scrolling
- [ ] CTA text describes the outcome, not the action (e.g. "Get your free assessment" not "Submit")
- [ ] Social proof or trust signal near the CTA
- [ ] Objection handling before the final CTA (FAQ, process explanation, guarantees)
- [ ] Minimal friction in the conversion path (fewer form fields, clear next steps)
- [ ] Mobile-optimized CTA placement and touch targets

## Conversion path design

1. **Awareness pages** (Home, Industries) → route visitors to evaluation pages
2. **Evaluation pages** (Solutions, Case Studies) → build understanding and trust
3. **Decision pages** (Contact, Pricing) → reduce friction and capture action
4. **Support pages** (FAQ, About) → handle objections and reinforce trust

## Measurement priorities

- Conversion rate per page
- CTA click-through rate
- Form completion rate
- Bounce rate on key landing pages
- Scroll depth on long-form pages

## Common conversion killers

- Vague or generic CTA ("Learn More", "Contact Us")
- No visible trust signal above the fold
- Too many competing CTAs on one page
- Form fields that feel invasive for the buying stage
- Missing "what happens next" after the CTA
