# Launch Rubric

Score each item from 1 to 5.

| Criterion                       | 1 (poor)                            | 3 (acceptable)                           | 5 (excellent)                                                   |
| ------------------------------- | ----------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Message consistency**         | Contradictions between pages        | Mostly consistent, minor drift           | All pages reinforce the same positioning and tone               |
| **Conversion readiness**        | No clear CTA or conversion path     | CTA exists but weak or inconsistent      | Strong, consistent CTA with clear conversion path on every page |
| **SEO readiness**               | No metadata, no keywords, no schema | Basic metadata, some keywords            | Full metadata, keywords mapped, schema implemented              |
| **Contact readiness**           | No contact page or broken form      | Contact page exists but friction-heavy   | Low-friction contact with clear next steps and confirmation     |
| **Trust and compliance**        | No proof, no legal pages            | Some proof elements, legal pages planned | Strong proof on key pages, legal pages published                |
| **Implementation completeness** | Major pages missing or broken       | All pages exist, some gaps               | All pages complete, tested, and matching business files         |
| **Technical SEO foundation**    | No robots.txt, sitemap, canonical   | Some present but incomplete              | robots.txt, sitemap.xml, canonical, OG, JSON-LD all correct     |

A site is not ready for launch below an average of 4.
