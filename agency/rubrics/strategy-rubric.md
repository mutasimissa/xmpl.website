# Strategy Rubric

Score each item from 1 to 5.

| Criterion                    | 1 (poor)                               | 3 (acceptable)                                          | 5 (excellent)                                           |
| ---------------------------- | -------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Audience clarity**         | No specific audience defined           | Audience named but vague                                | Specific role, context, and buying motivation described |
| **Positioning clarity**      | Generic or missing positioning         | Positioning exists but interchangeable with competitors | Unique, specific, and immediately clear                 |
| **Differentiation strength** | No differentiation or generic claims   | Some differentiators but not provable                   | Specific, concrete, and supportable with evidence       |
| **CTA alignment**            | No CTA or mismatched to business model | CTA exists but vague                                    | CTA is specific, outcome-oriented, and consistent       |
| **Message consistency**      | Messages contradict across files       | Mostly consistent with some gaps                        | All messages reinforce the same positioning             |
| **Proof readiness**          | No proof identified                    | Some proof types noted but not sourced                  | Specific proof with sources or a clear plan to obtain   |

A strategy output is not ready below an average of 4.
