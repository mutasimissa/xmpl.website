# Market / Location Page Blueprint

## Goal

Capture location-specific or market-specific search intent and demonstrate
relevance to a particular geographic or industry market.

## Recommended sections

1. Hero with market-specific headline and context
2. Market challenges and context (why this market matters)
3. Localized social proof (projects, clients, or stats from this market)
4. Service offerings relevant to this market
5. Process or approach adapted for this market
6. Local CTA (mention the market in the CTA)

## Inputs required

- business model
- page brief
- SEO brief (market-specific keywords)
- personas (adapted for this market)

## Internal linking

- CTA links to contact page
- Service mentions link to service pages
- Related market pages link to each other
- Proof section links to case studies

## SEO requirements

- H1 contains market name + service keyword
- Title tag: "[Service] in [Market] | Business Name"
- Meta description references the market specifically
- JSON-LD Service + location data if applicable
- LocalBusiness schema if the business has a physical presence

## Accessibility

- H1 for market-specific headline, H2 for sections
- Images have descriptive alt text with market context
- CTA buttons have descriptive text
