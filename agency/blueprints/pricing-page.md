# Pricing Page Blueprint

## Goal

Clarify packaging and reduce pricing uncertainty.

## Recommended sections

1. Pricing context
2. Packages or pricing model
3. What's included
4. Comparison or fit guidance
5. FAQ
6. CTA

## Inputs required

- business model (packaging, pricing logic)
- page brief
- personas (pricing objections)

## Internal linking

- Link to solutions page for capability details
- Link to contact page from CTA
- Link to FAQ for pricing-related questions

## Accessibility

- Pricing tables use proper table markup with headers
- Comparison features use accessible toggle or tab patterns
- CTA buttons have descriptive text
