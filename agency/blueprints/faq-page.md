# FAQ Page Blueprint

## Goal
Handle common buyer objections, reduce support burden, and capture informational search intent.

## Recommended sections
1. Introduction — what this page covers and who it's for
2. Pre-sale questions — about the business, offers, and fit
3. Process questions — how engagement works, timelines, deliverables
4. Pricing questions — cost structure, what's included, how to get a quote
5. Trust questions — credentials, experience, guarantees
6. Technical questions — integration, requirements, compatibility
7. CTA — route qualified visitors to contact

## Inputs required
- personas and objections
- business model
- value proposition
- SEO brief (FAQ keyword targets)

## Internal linking
- Link FAQ answers to relevant service/solution pages
- Link pricing questions to pricing page (if exists) or contact
- Link process questions to about page

## Accessibility
- Use proper `<details>` / `<summary>` or ARIA accordion pattern
- Ensure keyboard navigation between questions
- Consider FAQPage schema markup for rich results
