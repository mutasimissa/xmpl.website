# Blog Index Blueprint

## Goal

Showcase published content, encourage browsing, and route readers to relevant
posts based on their interests and buying stage.

## Recommended sections

1. Featured / latest post hero (large card with image, title, excerpt)
2. Category filter or tabs
3. Post grid — thumbnail, title, excerpt, date, category tag
4. Pagination or load-more
5. CTA sidebar or bottom section (newsletter signup or service link)

## Inputs required

- blog strategy (categories)
- brand strategy (tone)
- SEO brief (blog index keyword)

## Internal linking

- Each post card links to the full post
- Category filters link to filtered views
- CTA links to contact or service page
- Breadcrumb: Home > Blog

## SEO requirements

- H1: "Blog" or keyword-optimized variant
- Meta description: describes the blog's value proposition
- OG tags with website type
- JSON-LD BreadcrumbList

## Accessibility

- Post cards are keyboard-navigable
- Category filters are accessible (buttons or links, not just divs)
- Pagination uses proper nav element with aria-label
- Images have alt text
