# About Page Blueprint

## Goal

Increase trust, explain the company story, and support conversion.

## Recommended sections

1. Mission / positioning
2. Company background
3. What the company believes
4. Leadership or team
5. Proof / milestones
6. CTA

## Inputs required

- brand strategy
- business model
- value proposition

## Internal linking

- CTA links to contact page
- Proof section links to case studies
- Link to solutions page from company background

## Accessibility

- H1 for page title, H2 for each section
- Team photos have descriptive alt text
- CTA buttons have descriptive text
