# Local Landing Page Blueprint

## Goal

Capture local commercial intent while staying specific and credible.

## Recommended sections

1. Location-specific hero
2. Local problem context
3. Relevant offer summary
4. Trust and proof
5. FAQ
6. Contact CTA

## Inputs required

- business model
- brand strategy
- SEO brief (local keyword targets)
- location-specific information

## Internal linking

- Link to main solutions page
- Link to contact page from CTA
- Link to relevant case studies in the region

## Accessibility

- Location information in text (not only in images/maps)
- Embedded maps have text alternatives
- CTA buttons have descriptive text
