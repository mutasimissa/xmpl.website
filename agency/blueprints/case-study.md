# Case Study Blueprint

## Goal

Show proof through a before / after business story.

## Recommended sections

1. Client summary
2. Challenge
3. Approach
4. Solution delivered
5. Outcome
6. Quote or proof points
7. CTA

## Inputs required

- business model
- brand strategy
- client information and results

## Internal linking

- Link to relevant service/solution page
- Link to contact page from CTA
- Link to other case studies for social proof reinforcement

## Accessibility

- H1 for case study title, H2 for each section
- Data visualizations have text alternatives
- Quote blocks use proper `blockquote` markup
