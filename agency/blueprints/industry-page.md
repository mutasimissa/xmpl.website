# Industry Page Blueprint

## Goal
Show relevance to a specific industry segment, build trust through context awareness, and route visitors to solutions or contact.

## Recommended sections
1. Industry-specific hero — name the industry and its core challenge
2. Problem context — what teams in this industry struggle with
3. Relevant solutions — how the business solves industry-specific problems
4. Proof — case studies, references, or credentials in this industry
5. Differentiators — why this business is a strong fit for this industry
6. FAQ — industry-specific objections
7. CTA — route to contact or solutions page

## Inputs required
- business model
- personas (industry-specific needs)
- brand strategy
- SEO brief (industry keyword targets)

## Internal linking
- Link to solutions page for detailed offer information
- Link to case studies relevant to this industry
- Link to contact page with industry-specific context

## Accessibility
- Use semantic headings for each section
- Ensure industry-specific images have descriptive alt text
