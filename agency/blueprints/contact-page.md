# Contact Page Blueprint

## Goal

Reduce friction and make conversion obvious.

## Recommended sections

1. Clear invitation
2. Reasons to contact
3. Form or direct channels
4. What happens next
5. Trust or reassurance

## Inputs required

- business model (conversion event)
- page brief
- brand strategy (CTA logic)

## Internal linking

- Link back to solutions page for visitors still evaluating
- Link to FAQ for visitors with remaining questions
- Link to about page for trust-seeking visitors

## Accessibility

- Form inputs have associated labels
- Required fields indicated accessibly (not by color alone)
- Error messages are clear and associated with their field
- Submit button has descriptive text
- Success/error states announced to screen readers
